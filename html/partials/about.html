About Us

Artists' Forum is one of the hobby clubs in NITK. As the name itself suggests, it's the perfect place for all those budding engineers who portray a great passion in fine arts and craft.

Famous as AF in NITK we are a group of creative, fun loving, enthusiastic people who are from diverse backgrounds united by their one true passion - art. Be it origami, kirigami, 3D painting, painting, sketching, quilling, or any other form, AF houses it all!

Over the years AF has become more of a family than a club and this is a testimony to the bonds we have formed with each other. AF is a place where innovation is not only appreciated, but encouraged.
